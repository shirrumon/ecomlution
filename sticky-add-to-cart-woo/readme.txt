Simple Sticky Add To Cart For WooCommerce helps you to get more sales and conversion with unique and elegant way. This Plugin adds sticky add to cart on Product page. this button appears when a user scroll down in single product page, it keeps the add to cart button in front of your customers eyes and keeps encouraging them to make a purchase. 

Some products have long content. When you finish the reading often you find add the cart button getting hidden and customers end up ignoring the add to cart button. but if you have the Simple Sticky Add To Cart it will not let the Add to cart button hidden.
